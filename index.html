KEY MAPPING

  • DEL Backspace | Delete
  • AC Esc | End
  • EQUAL Enter
  • NEGATIVE ( - ) Shift + [ - ]
  • INFO/CALC FLIP PgUp | PgDn | I
  • Numbers and operators can be accessed through the numpad and number row keys

LIMITATIONS

  • This is a Calculator portfolio project, such that it has limited functions, thus the name Poor Man's Calculator.
  • I have limited the characters for the input screen to 19 and answer screen to 12.
  • Answers with large floating point will be rounded to accommodate the answer screen limit.
  • Meanwhile, large numbers will be converted to exponential form.
  • I have disabled large numbers for chaining operations and will alert the user with a "Memory Full" message.
